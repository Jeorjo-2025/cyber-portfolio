CISSP Security Governance Principles & Knowledge Hub

Core: Security & Risk Management (Domain 1)

The core of Domain 1 focuses on establishing a framework for effective security management across the organization.

1. Alignment with Business Objectives

  • Strategic Alignment: Security must support the organization's mission and goals (business enabler).
  • Tone at the Top: Senior management sets strategy, funding and ensures commitment.
  • Organizational Processes: Integrate security into acquisitions, outsourcing and third-party management.

2. Roles, Responsibilities & Accountability

  • Accountability vs Responsibility: Data Owner (accountable), Data Custodian (responsible for implementation).
  • Key Roles: Data Owner, Data Custodian, Senior Management duties and handoffs.

3. Foundational Concepts & Due Diligence

  • CIA Triad: Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability.
  • Due Care: Acting prudently in security decisions; the "reasonable person" standard.
  • Due Diligence: Ongoing effort (audits, scanning) to maintain due care.

4. Documentation Framework

  • Policy: High-level mandatory statements ("what" and "why").
  • Standards: Mandatory technical specs ("how").
  • Procedures: Step-by-step instructions.
  • Guidelines: Recommended practices.

5. Legal, Regulatory & Ethics

  • Compliance: GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, and industry standards.
  • ISC2 Code of Ethics: Professional conduct obligations.
